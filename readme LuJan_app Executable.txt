LuJan_app Executable

1. Prerequisites for Deployment 
- LuJan_app.exe
- Matlab installed


For the prerequisites:
The LuJan_app can be installed directly in Matlab (LuJan_app.mlappinstall). 


Instructions of use
- The LuJan_app offers interactive, user-friendly, and straightforward tools to generate customized 3D nerve fiber trajectories. For creating customized trajectories, 
  - slide the fiber Interval, or
  - slide the path movement, or
  - select right or left eye, or
  - create a tail by clicking the tail box.
